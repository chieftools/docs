## Documentation

The first place you've already found, this <ExternalLink href="https://docs.chief.tools">documentation site</ExternalLink>. We try to document common cases and answer frequently asked questions about the tools we provide.

## Roadmap

We have a public roadmap where our users can vote on features and suggest new ones. You can find it at <ExternalLink href="https://roadmap.chief.tools">roadmap.chief.tools</ExternalLink> and login with your Chief Tools account to participate.

## Contact us

If you have a question that is not answered here, need help with something or have a private matter, please <ExternalLink href="https://chief.app/contact">contact us</ExternalLink> and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.
